Frequently asked questions

What is an NFT?

A Non-Fungible Token (NFT) is a digital asset, including art and digital collectibles, real estate, gaming, and more that lives on the blockchain. They certify ownership, so no two NFTs are the same because they have different artistic properties. Like physical art, they can be copied, but not replicated since there is only one true owner of the original piece.

How do you make and sell NFTs?

You can use the BitPay plugin for easy set up. Then you'll need to set up your non-custodial wallet with BitPay to hold your NFTs. Now you can manage your NFT collection and upload your art. Since NFTs live on the blockchain, you will need to pay to tokenize your art. Ethereum is the most common blockchain. Now you are able to list your art with BitPay to sell your NFTs to crypto enthusiasts.

How can businesses use NFTs?

Many businesses have started using NFTs to help create and build brand awareness and increase engagement with customers. It's also a great way to create a loyal fanbase by allowing your consumers to buy a unique digital asset that only they own. Some examples are PacSun, Coca-Cola, Pringles, and Taco Bell.

How do I use BitPay to accept Bitcoin and cryptocurrency payments?

With BitPay, your business can accept cryptocurrency payments online or in-store. Online sales are made via eCommerce plugins, website payment buttons, code libraries, or direct API implementation. In-person payments occur on integrated point-of-sale terminals or the BitPay Checkout app. This creates a fast, hassle-free checkout experience for customers and staff. Additionally, merchants can create invoices and receive payment with BitPay's email billing.

What are BitPay's fees?

BitPay typically charges a flat 1% fee on each payment. There are no set up fees or monthly minimums.

What cryptocurrency coins does BitPay support?

BitPay supports Bitcoin, major alt coins, tokens, and stable coins. We are constantly evaluating and adding new coins. Currently we support:

  • Bitcoin (BTC)
  • Ethereum (ETH)
  • Bitcoin Cash (BCH)
  • Dogecoin (DOGE)
  • Shiba Inu (SHIB)
  • Litecoin (LTC)
  • XRP (XRP)
  • Dai (DAI)
  • Binance USD (BUSD)
  • USD Coin (USDC)
  • Wrapped Bitcoin (WBTC)
  • Pax Dollar (USDP)
  • Gemini Dollar (GUSD)
Why should I choose BitPay as my crypto payment processor?

BitPay has processed over $5 billion in transactions since 2011. Main street brands and large corporations continually choose BitPay as their crypto payment processor. We offer businesses the power to accept crypto payments without any risk or volatility. Our processing service is compatible with almost any wallet, ensuring a safe and secure transaction. Other payment processors require the use of their own custodial wallets greatly limiting your global reach. The variety of methods to send and accept payments gives businesses ultimate flexibility. Additionally, the low fees are below or comparable to typical credit card transaction fees.
